MY JOURNEYS

BARCELONA

"We crossed spacious streets, with building resembling palaces,
in La Rambla promenade ;
the shops were well illuminated and there was movement and life...
I did not decide to go to sleep, even though I wished to, so I could rise early and contemplate, in daylight, this city, unknown to me:
Barcelona, capital of Catalonia"

- HANS CHRISTIAN ANDERSEN
